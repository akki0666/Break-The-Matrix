About Us

Break The Matrix is a podcast designed to challenge conventional thinking and explore transformative ideas that can reshape the way we view the world. Through engaging conversations, thought-provoking topics, and diverse panelists, we dive into areas such as mental health, personal growth, technology, culture, and more.

Want to Become Our Next Panelist?

Do you have unique insights, captivating stories, or expertise in areas that can inspire and empower others? We’re always on the lookout for passionate individuals to join us as panelists on Break The Matrix. This is your opportunity to share your perspective and contribute to meaningful discussions that challenge norms and inspire growth.

How to Apply:


  • Provide Your Details: Fill out the provided Google Form with your information, including your background, area of expertise, and what inspires you to be part of our podcast.

  • Highlight Your Expertise: Share any relevant experiences, projects, or topics you’d like to discuss on the show.

  • Be Part of the Movement: As a panelist, you’ll have the chance to connect with like-minded individuals, share your knowledge, and leave a lasting impact on our listeners.
